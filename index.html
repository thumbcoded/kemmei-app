What is Kemmei? Kemmei is an offline desktop flashcard study/test app for CompTIA certifications. Currently, it covers A+ Core 1 (220-1201), with plans to expand to Core 2 and other titles depending on user feedback and demand.

How your data is stored: User progress and metadata are stored locally using localStorage and a packaged user database. Test results, deck completions, and unlocks are tied to your chosen username and saved per-user.

Flashcards structure: Kemmei flashcards are organized by domain and subdomain, following the official CompTIA exam objectives.

Deck selection & Test mode: Choose a deck by domain and subdomain, then select a Mode: Casual or Test. Test mode records your result; completing a deck with at least 90% correct answers unlocks the next difficulty level. Casual mode does not record results but preserves your current unlocks.
